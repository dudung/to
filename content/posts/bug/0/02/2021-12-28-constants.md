## gravitational constant
Konstanta gravitasi [[1](#r01)]

\begin{equation}
G = 6.674 \ 30(15) \times 10^{-11} \ {\rm m^3 \cdot kg^{-1} \cdot s^{-2}}.
\end{equation}
