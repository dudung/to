## Summary (1 of 5)
+ Frequency
  - 1 Hz = 1 cyle per second
+ Period
  - $$\tag{15-2}
    T = \frac1f
    $$
+ Simple Harmonic Motion
  - Find $v$ and $a$ by differentiation
  $$\tag{15-3}
  x(t) = x_m \cos(\omega t + \phi)
  $$
  $$\tag{15-5}
  \omega = \frac{2\pi}{T} = 2\pi f.
  $$
+ The Linear Oscillator.
  - $$\tag{15-12}
  \omega = \sqrt{\frac{k}{m}}
  $$
  - $$\tag{15-13}
  T = 2\pi \sqrt{\frac{m}{k}}
  $$
